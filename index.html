No Stress, One-on-One Dog Grooming in a Private Home Studio

Pawfact Grooming is a single-member, private one-on-one home-based dog spa serving Bellevue, Seattle, and nearby areas. We prioritize your dog’s comfort and safety while providing personalized grooming services tailored to each individual.

With a gentle and loving approach, I strive to create a stress-free environment where your dog can feel completely at ease. To enhance relaxation, all grooming sessions are conducted with soothing music in the background.

At Pawfact Grooming, every dog receives premium-quality shampoos, ensuring the best care for their coat. During drying, dogs wear a Happy Hood to reduce noise and promote calmness. Grooming sessions are conducted with patience and precision, typically lasting up to 2 hours for dogs weighing up to 45 lbs and up to 3 hours for dogs weighing up to 80 lbs, depending on the breed.

I am proud to have completed the AKC S.A.F.E. Grooming Program and hold a Professional Pet Groomer Certification, reflecting my commitment to the highest standards of pet care.
